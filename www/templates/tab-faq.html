DOES FIND MY SUPER GUARANTEE A RESULT?

Find my super cannot guarantee a result. We rely on the accuracy of your data as well as the accuracy coming from the superannuation funds. All superannuation funds have to report contributions with a tax file number. If the tax file number matches then we are more likely to get a positive result.
